FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free pickup and drop?+
Yes! Free pickup and drop is available across Chennai for Gold and Platinum package customers. Bronze and Silver customers can avail this service at a nominal fee. Call 9342787621 to schedule.
Which superbike brands do you service?+
We service BMW Motorrad, Ducati, Kawasaki, Triumph, Aprilia, MV Agusta, Suzuki Hayabusa, Honda Fireblade, KTM, Yamaha R1/R6, Benelli, Harley-Davidson, Indian Motorcycle, Bimota, Norton, and more — 23+ brands in total.
How long does a service take?+
Basic service takes 2–4 hours. Full service takes 4–8 hours. ECU tuning and performance work may take 1–2 days depending on complexity. We will give you an exact time estimate before starting.
Do you use genuine parts?+
Yes! We use only OEM genuine parts and certified aftermarket parts. We never compromise on quality. All parts come with manufacturer warranty and are documented in your digital health report.
What is the Digital Health Report?+
After every service, you receive a comprehensive PDF health report on WhatsApp showing Engine condition, Brake status, Tyre life %, Battery health, Chain condition, and next service due date — with before & after photos.
Do you offer 24/7 emergency support?+
Yes! Call 9342787621 or 8939618461 anytime for emergency breakdown support. You can also share your location on WhatsApp and we will come to assist you anywhere in Chennai.
How do I track my bike service status?+
Use our Service Tracker on this website — enter your mobile number to see real-time status. We also send WhatsApp updates at every stage of the service automatically.
Can you import parts for my bike?+
Yes! REVORA has a dedicated Import & Export division sourcing parts from USA, Europe, Japan, Thailand, and UAE. We can source exhausts, carbon fiber parts, ECU modules, suspension components, riding gear, and more.
